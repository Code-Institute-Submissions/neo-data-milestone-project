Near Earth Object Close Approach Data Visualisation

This website takes data from the freely available NASA API: Neo-Feed. This API retrieves a list of Near Earth Objects (NEO's) based on their close approach date to Earth over a period of 1 week from a specified start date. A data dashboard is created from the information received from the API, the focus of which are data visualisations based upon the estimated maximum diameter of the NEO; it's close approach distance to Earth; and whether the NEO is potentially hazardous to Earth. It should be noted that the NEO's potential hazard to Earth is not specifically based upon whether it is immediately hazardous to Earth but it may be at a later date. Create the data visualisations by entering a start date for the search then press the 'Start Search' button to generate the data dashboard.
